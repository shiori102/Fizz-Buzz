What does FizzBuzz do?

This application creates an array of numbers. The user will enter in a range of numbers and display it onto a table. The application will replace numbers that are in multiles of 3 to print "Fizz." And for the multiples of five, the application will display "Buzz." For the numbers of five and three, it will display "FizzBuzz."
